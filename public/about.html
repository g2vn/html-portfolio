About Me

Who am I?

I'm an aspiring web developer with a passion in UI/UX design, learning to craft beautiful, fluid websites and design attractive, gripping websites that can pull any user and client in.
With a heart for art, drive for coding and a love for tech, combining the three most essential pieces of my life comes together in the form of chasing my career goal.
Join me as I venture deeper into the world of coding, design and web development.

What Have I Done?

I am a 20 year old attending the University of Houston-Downtown, where I am reaching for a Bachelors in Computer Science, with an outside focus on web development and design.
I've worked closely with rising Houston tech collective, The Ion, as a student ambassador, engaging in various group sessions, trips and meetings to expand my knowledge of coding, marketing and networking all at once.
I also have been apart of on-campus groups, CAHSI and ACM, computing groups geared towards the development of Computer Science students alike.

Growing up...

Tech was new and unraveling, and it fascinated me. I took Robotics in my middle school class and, needless to say, it was eyeopening and I realized from a young age that I was fascinated with the industry and needed more.
Throughout the years I've garnered skills and am constantly making the push to increase my knowledge as a tech lover and as a programmer alike.
