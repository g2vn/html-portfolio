The Best Movies Ever...According to Me!

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Chronicle

A movie filled with supernatural phenomena, average writing and the impressive camera work takes it's place on my list
It's filled with some beautiful shots and steady color palette and a decent cast.
Though it's not seen as the best in a lot of eyes, the subject matter of powers used in this way is realistic and interesting
Bravo!

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Real themes, real people.
With a star-studded cast, an inventive director, artists perfection, this film embodies so much of what cinema is.
The colors pop, the actors are embedded int their ole, and best of all, the art and themes of the scenes are shockingly perfect.
One of the greatest films to grace our world, truly.

Soul

Soul. A movie that encapsulates the beautiful path of life and death, in the most comforting way yet.
Following the story of Joe Gardener, exploring death, life, blackness, passion, it's all very home-hitting.
this movies direction in terms of approach orally and artistically blew me away, and I actually cried watching this.
A beautiful film that has yet to be topped in my heart.
